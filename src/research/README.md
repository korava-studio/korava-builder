# Research & Evolution Engine

The Research Engine collects and analyzes technology signals, generates reports, and updates the knowledge system.

## Components

- `engine/ResearchEngine` — core research orchestration
- `sources/SourceRegistry` — registers research sources
- `scheduler/ResearchScheduler` — schedules research cycles
- `analyzer/TrendAnalyzer` — produces insights and assessments
- `reports/ReportGenerator` — generates research reports
- `knowledge/KnowledgeUpdater` — emits knowledge updates from approved reports
